How do I buy an AstroBear NFT?

1. Install the MetaMask Chrome or Firefox extension.

2. Create your personal wallet via MetaMask.

3. Use Coinbase, Binance, FTX, Crypto.com, or Kraken to convert your money to ETH andsend it to your newly created wallet.

4. Click the button "MINT" on the top of this website to connect your wallet (July 20th) and approve the transaction on MetaMask.

5. After approval your AstroBear NFT will be visible in your wallet and on OpenSea
